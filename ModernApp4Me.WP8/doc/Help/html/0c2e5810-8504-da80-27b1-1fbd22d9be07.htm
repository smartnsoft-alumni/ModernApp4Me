M4MResolutionBitmapImageConverter
Enables to choose the best BitmapImage from the assets according to device resolution. This class uses the M4MResolutionImageChooser to take its choice. The parameter should be something like "Path/to/Image/ImageName-Resolution.Extension" where resolution can be HD, WXGA or WVGA.
